Paddy Information

Pre-Planting (Before Sowing)

  • Timing: 1-2 Weeks Before Sowing
  • Fertilizer: Organic Manure (Compost/FYM)
  • Purpose: Improve soil structure, increase nutrient availability
  • Recommended Quantity: 5-10 tons/ha
  • Diseases: Blast, Bacterial Leaf Blight

Transplanting Stage

  • Timing: At Sowing
  • Fertilizer: Starter Fertilizer (N-P-K)
  • Purpose: Provide balanced nutrients for initial growth
  • Recommended Quantity: 20-30 kg/ha

Vegetative Growth (Tillering Stage)

  • Timing: 3-4 Weeks After Sowing
  • Fertilizer: Nitrogen (N) (Top-Dressing)
  • Purpose: Support vegetative growth and tiller formation
  • Recommended Quantity: 40-60 kg/ha
  • Pest: Brown Planthopper

Reproductive Stage (Panicle Initiation)

  • Timing: 6-8 Weeks After Sowing
  • Fertilizer: Phosphorus (P), Potassium (K)
  • Purpose: Promote grain formation and filling
  • Recommended Quantity: 30-40 kg/ha
  • Diseases: Stem Borer, Sheath Blight

Maturity Stage

  • Timing: 10+ Weeks After Sowing
  • Fertilizer: No Further Fertilization
  • Purpose: Allow grains to mature, prepare for harvest
